## Decisions Made

- **Kept `Write` in the skill's `tools` list** (Read, Write, Task, Bash) per the plan's `<interfaces>` block rationale. The plan explicitly said `Write` could be omitted "if omitting `Write` passes size-budget + lint more cleanly" — but the skill is 82 lines (well under the 90-line target), markdownlint is green, and keeping `Write` preserves forward compatibility with the proposal→confirm extensions the plan's rationale block foresees.
- **`--refresh` and `--since` are mutually exclusive** with an explicit error message: `--refresh and --since are mutually exclusive. --refresh re-seeds the snapshot silently; --since surfaces a backlog from a boundary date. Pick one.` This implements the plan's Step 1 "Mutual exclusion rules" second bullet. Semantically: letting both be set would mean "seed silently AND surface everything since X", which is self-contradictory.
- **`--since` validation uses portable parse** — skill body documents both `date -d "<value>" +%s` (GNU coreutils, works on most Linux CI runners) and `python3 -c "from datetime import datetime; datetime.fromisoformat('<value>'.replace('Z','+00:00'))"` (POSIX-portable, works on macOS where GNU `date -d` is absent). Either works; implementer picks based on runtime.
- **Cron expressions locked at 09:00 local time** — `weekly=0 9 * * 1` (Mondays), `daily=0 9 * * *` (every day), `monthly=0 9 1 * *` (1st of the month). 09:00 is the standard "start-of-day for a design sprint" slot and matches the cadence-hint convention in `reference/authority-feeds.md`.
- **Root SKILL.md wiring registers watch-authorities as a first-class /gdd:* command** — not a sub-skill or hidden command. Pattern: `/gdd:watch-authorities → Skill("get-design-done:gdd-watch-authorities")` jump-mode entry under "Audit & Session" alongside `/gdd:reflect` and `/gdd:apply-reflections` (the skills in the same improvement loop). Command-table row placed immediately after `/gdd:skill-manifest` — tail of the maintenance group, just before the Handoff Routing section.
